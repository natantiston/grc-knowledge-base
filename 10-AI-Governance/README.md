# Chapter 10 – Artificial Intelligence (AI) Governance & ISO/IEC 42001:2023

## Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the way organizations operate, make decisions, and deliver products and services. As AI adoption continues to accelerate, organizations must establish governance mechanisms that ensure AI systems are trustworthy, secure, ethical, transparent, and compliant with applicable laws and regulations.

This chapter introduces the principles of Artificial Intelligence Governance and provides a comprehensive guide to implementing an Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS) based on **ISO/IEC 42001:2023**, the world's first international management system standard for AI.

Readers will learn how to govern AI throughout its lifecycle, manage AI-specific risks, implement responsible AI practices, protect privacy and security, conduct AI audits, respond to AI incidents, and build an enterprise-wide AI governance program.

The chapter combines internationally recognized standards, governance best practices, and practical implementation guidance suitable for organizations of all sizes and industries.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:

- Understand the principles and objectives of AI governance.
- Explain the purpose and structure of ISO/IEC 42001:2023.
- Implement an Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS).
- Apply governance throughout the AI lifecycle.
- Identify, assess, and manage AI-related risks.
- Establish responsible and ethical AI practices.
- Protect AI systems through appropriate security controls.
- Manage privacy and personal data in AI applications.
- Compare ISO/IEC 42001 with other major AI governance frameworks.
- Maintain AI governance documentation, inventories, and registers.
- Respond effectively to AI incidents and model failures.
- Conduct AI internal audits and compliance audits.
- Prepare an organization for ISO/IEC 42001 certification.
- Design an enterprise AI governance program with executive oversight.
- Measure governance effectiveness using KPIs, KRIs, and executive dashboards.

# Key Takeaways

This chapter provides a complete guide to modern AI governance based on ISO/IEC 42001:2023. It covers governance principles, AI lifecycle management, ethics, security, privacy, compliance, auditing, certification, and enterprise governance structures. By applying the concepts presented throughout this chapter, organizations can establish a robust Artificial Intelligence Management System that enables responsible AI adoption, manages AI-related risks, satisfies regulatory expectations, and supports continual improvement while maintaining stakeholder trust.
