# Overview

Welcome to **Chapter 4** of the **GRC Knowledge Base**.

This chapter introduces **ISO/IEC 27001:2022**, the world's leading international standard for Information Security Management Systems (ISMS). It provides a structured, risk-based framework for establishing, implementing, operating, monitoring, reviewing, maintaining, and continually improving information security across an organization. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Unlike technical cybersecurity standards, ISO/IEC 27001 focuses on **governance, leadership, risk management, and continual improvement**, ensuring that information security becomes part of the organization's overall business strategy.

Whether you are preparing for ISO/IEC 27001 certification, working as a GRC professional, implementing an ISMS, or preparing for certification exams such as **CISM**, **CISA**, **CRISC**, or **ISO 27001 Lead Implementer/Lead Auditor**, this chapter provides the knowledge needed to understand how an ISMS is designed, implemented, and maintained.
