# Chapter 07 – ISO/IEC 42001 Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS)

Welcome to **Chapter 07** of the **GRC Knowledge Base**.

This chapter provides a comprehensive guide to **ISO/IEC 42001:2023**, the world's first international management system standard specifically designed for **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**. As organizations increasingly adopt AI to automate business processes, improve decision-making, and deliver innovative products and services, effective governance becomes essential to ensure AI systems are developed and used responsibly, securely, ethically, and in compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.

ISO/IEC 42001 introduces the **Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS)**, enabling organizations to establish governance structures, manage AI risks, define responsibilities, implement operational controls, monitor performance, and drive continual improvement throughout the AI lifecycle.

Whether you are an AI Governance professional, Cybersecurity Consultant, GRC Practitioner, Risk Manager, Compliance Officer, Internal Auditor, Data Scientist, AI Engineer, or Executive Leader, this chapter provides both the theoretical foundation and practical implementation guidance needed to successfully design, implement, audit, and continually improve an Artificial Intelligence Management System.

# Chapter Summary

Artificial Intelligence presents tremendous opportunities for innovation, efficiency, and competitive advantage—but it also introduces new risks related to ethics, security, privacy, transparency, accountability, bias, and regulatory compliance. ISO/IEC 42001 provides organizations with a structured management system for governing these risks while enabling responsible AI adoption.

By completing this chapter, you have learned how to establish, implement, maintain, monitor, audit, and continually improve an Artificial Intelligence Management System using internationally recognized best practices. More importantly, you have developed the practical knowledge required to integrate AI governance into broader Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) programs and prepare organizations for successful ISO/IEC 42001 certification.

As AI continues to evolve, organizations that adopt strong governance practices will be better positioned to innovate responsibly, earn stakeholder trust, meet regulatory expectations, and build resilient, future-ready AI ecosystems.
