# What is Risk Management?

## Introduction

Risk management is the systematic process of identifying, assessing, treating, monitoring, and communicating risks that could affect an organization's objectives.

Effective risk management enables organizations to make informed decisions, reduce uncertainty, and improve resilience.

---

## Risk Management Process

1. Identify Risks
2. Analyze Risks
3. Evaluate Risks
4. Treat Risks
5. Monitor Risks
6. Communicate and Review

---

## Why Risk Management Matters

- Protects business objectives
- Supports strategic decision-making
- Reduces financial losses
- Improves regulatory compliance
- Strengthens organizational resilience
- Enhances stakeholder confidence

---

## Related Standards

- ISO 31000
- ISO 27005
- ISO 27001
- NIST Risk Management Framework

---

## Summary

Risk management is a continuous process that helps organizations balance opportunities and threats while achieving business objectives.
